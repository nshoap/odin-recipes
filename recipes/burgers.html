Burgers

Description

Burgers, specifically cheeseburgers, are a favorite among Americans. You can't drive through a town without seeing a burger shop. And it's no wonder; burgers are delicious. Although there are many different styles, a personal favorite of mine is the smashburger.

Smashburgers are 2-4oz patties, smashed down ultra-thin, and then covered in cheese. This results in a nice, crispy crust on the outside of the burger, with a juicy patty and gooey cheese.

Ingredients

  • Sesame-seed buns
  • About 3oz of ground beef per patty
  • American cheese
  • Salt and Pepper

Recipe steps

  1. Bring a lightly oiled cast-iron skillet to medium heat
  2. Grab about 3oz of meat, roll into a ball
  3. Insert patty, smash down patty as thin as possible
  4. Season the patty, cook for about 60-90 seconds on one side
  5. Flip, top with cheese
  6. Cook for another 60-90 seconds or until done
  7. Remove patty, plate on bun, top as desired, enjoy
