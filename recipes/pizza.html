Pizza

Description

New York Style Pizza is one of the most popular styles, and is a favorite of many. It is known for its thin, wide slices that generally have minimal toppings, often just cheese and pepperoni. Nonetheless, it is considered one of the best pizzas. This web page will walk you through how to make a New York Style pie from the comfort of your own home

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Whole-milk, low-moisture mozzarella cheese
  • Any additional toppings you wish

Recipe steps

  1. In a large bowl, pour 200ml of lukewarm water
  2. Mix in about a tablespoon of sugar
  3. Mix in about a tablespoon of olive oil
  4. Add a pinch of yeast, about 0.25oz
  5. Mix in flour until it begins to become cohesive
  6. Mix together, let rest
  7. Add some olive oil to separate containers
  8. Divide dough ball into quarters
  9. Spread the oil around with the dough ball, oiling the ball and container
  10. Allow dough to cold-rise for several days
  11. While dough is still cold, spread out to desired size and shape
  12. Top with sauce, cheese, additional toppings
  13. Cook at highest temp for 6-10 minutes
